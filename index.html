Muscle Well physiotherapy is aimed at those who have problems, pain or injury in movement/support equipment. We provide you with a professional physiotherapeutic assessment and treatment as well as information and help for self-care after the end of the treatment period.

We tailor assignments to your specific needs. Have a stationary reception centrally located in Mellansel, Framtidens Hus, but we can also come to you and carry out treatment and training in your home environment.

Availability in rural areas, many years and broad experience as a physiotherapist. Our goal is to offer physiotherapy interventions with high quality, good service, short waiting times and good treatment.

Muscle Well is an independent physiotherapy practice from the county council.
