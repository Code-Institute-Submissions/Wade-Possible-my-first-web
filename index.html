Service

Muscle Well physiotherapy is aimed at those who have problems, pain or injury in movement/support equipment. I provide you with a professional physiotherapeutic assessment and treatment as well as information and help for self-care after the end of the treatment period.

I tailor assignments to your specific needs. Have a stationary reception centrally located in Mellansel, Framtidens Hus, but I can also come to you and carry out treatment and training in your home environment.

Availability in rural areas, many years and broad experience as a physiotherapist. My goal is to offer physiotherapy interventions with high quality, good service, short waiting times and good treatment.

Toppform fysioterapia AB is an independent physiotherapy practice from the county council.
